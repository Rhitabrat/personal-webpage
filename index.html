I am a PhD Student in Computer Science (Natural Language Processing) at Portland State University, under the guidance of Dr. Ameeta Agrawal (PortNLP Lab). Currently, I am working on large language models (LLMs) and low resource languages.

I am open to collaboration on topics related to low-resource languages, machine translation (MT), MT post-editing (MTPE), idiomaticity, multi-word expressions, social media information extraction or anything pertaining to NLP and ML. I am in the job market for Research Internship for the summer of 2024.
